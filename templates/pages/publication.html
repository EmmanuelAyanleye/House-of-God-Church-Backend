The Publications Department was established in 1998, with its primary responsibility being the meticulous transcription of church sermons from audio recordings into well-edited text, preparing them for distribution as publications such as comprehensive books or insightful articles, as per the directives of the Pastor.

Additionally, the Department is tasked with the creative responsibility of assigning appropriate titles to church sermons and overseeing the production of the esteemed "Householder" magazine.

The Holy Police Department was established in 1988. It is responsible for ensuring the security of cars parked in the churchâ€™s car park while members attend worship services. The department also manages traffic during church activities and warmly welcomes members at the parking area, ensuring a smooth flow of vehicles.

Beyond this, the department is tasked with maintaining the safety of the entire church premises. They ensure a secure environment for worshippers, providing a sense of safety and peace during services. Their duties also include protecting church property from vandalism or unauthorized access, maintaining the sanctity of the holy grounds.

The department is composed of approximately 20 committed members who volunteer their time and efforts.

They convene on the last Saturday of each month to discuss security strategies, review their responsibilities, and plan for upcoming church events.

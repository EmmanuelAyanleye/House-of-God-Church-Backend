The Villa Sanitation Department was formed and inaugurated in 1998. The department is responsible for the thorough cleaning of the Church auditorium, ensuring that the space is always neat, with curtains properly arranged and chairs neatly positioned.

Their duties extend to overseeing the preparation of the Communion elements, ensuring they are ready and properly administered during both regular services and wedding ceremonies.

The Villa Sanitation Department is a dedicated team of approximately 100 members, comprising both men and women who serve diligently. Their scheduled meeting days are as follows: Wednesdays (6:00 am – 9:00 am) for general cleaning, Fridays (4:00 pm – 6:00 pm) for preparation ahead of weekend activities, Saturdays (12:00 noon until the end of all church activities), and Sundays (only on the first Sunday of each month) specifically for Communion preparations. Their commitment ensures that the church remains clean, welcoming, and well-prepared for all events.
